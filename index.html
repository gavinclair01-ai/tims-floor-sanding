What We Do

Complete Timber Floor Services

From dusty old floorboards to gleaming showroom finish — we handle the full job, start to finish.

🪵

Floor Sanding

We strip back years of wear, scratches and old finishes to reveal the natural beauty of your timber. Dust-minimised equipment keeps your home clean.

✨

Polishing & Finishing

Choose from matte, satin or gloss finishes. We use premium water-based and oil-based polyurethanes for a durable, stunning result that lasts.

🔧

Floor Restoration

Got gaps, squeaks, damaged boards or stains? We repair and restore your existing floors — often at a fraction of the cost of replacement.

🎨

Staining & Colouring

Transform your floors with a custom stain — from warm honey tones to rich dark walnut. We match your vision and the style of your home.

🏠

New Floor Finishing

Just installed new timber floors? We prepare and finish them perfectly so they're protected and looking their best from day one.

🏢

Commercial Projects

Offices, restaurants, retail spaces and more. We work around your hours — including nights and weekends — to minimise business disruption.
